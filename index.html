Bear loves his favourite Marmalade sandwich when he is coding. Here is how you make it step-by-step:
  1. Gather Ingredients: You will need two slices of bread and marmalade.
  2. Prepare the Workspace: Clear and clean your workspace, and have a plate ready to assemble your sandwich.
  3. Spread Marmalade: Take one slice of bread and spread a generous amount of marmalade on one side of it. Adjust the amount of marmalade to your taste preference.
  4. Top with Second Bread Slice: Place the second slice of bread on top of the marmalade spread to complete the sandwich.
  5. Optional Toasting: If you prefer your marmalade sandwich toasted, you can toast the bread slices in a toaster before spreading the marmalade. Alternatively, you can grill the sandwich in a sandwich maker after assembly.
  6. Serve: Place your marmalade sandwich on a plate. If not toasted, you might want to cut it in half or quarters for easier eating.
  7. Enjoy: Your simple marmalade sandwich is ready to eat. Enjoy it as a quick snack or breakfast treat.
